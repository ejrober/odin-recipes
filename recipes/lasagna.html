Beefwellington

Description

Lasagna.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Ground beef
  • Cheese
  • Tomato Sauce

Steps

  1. Bring 4 quarts of water to a boil and add the noodles.
  2. In another pan brown the ground beef.
  3. When the noodles are aldente strain out the water.
  4. In a baking dish sprayed with no stick spray add a layer of noodles and ground beef. Repeat the layers until you are out of ingredients.
  5. Top with cheese and bake for 30 to 45 minutes at 400 degrees farenheit.
