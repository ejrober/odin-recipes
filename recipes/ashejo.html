Ash-e Jo

Description

Ash-e Jo.

Ingredients

  • 1 large onion diced
  • 4 tablespoons olive oil
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 teaspoon tumeric
  • 2 teaspoon cumin
  • 1.2L chicken or vegetable broth
  • 700g jar red kidney beans, including liquid
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 cup pearled barley, soaked overnight
  • 1/2 cup white basmati rice, rinsed

Steps

  1. First prepare the beef tenderloin by seasoning it with salt and pepper.
  2. Next sear the beef tenderloin in a hot pan with butter
  3. Once the beef tenderloin is seared on all sides we can prepare the pastry.
  4. With the pastry prepared we wrap the beef tenderloin in the pastry and bake.

Ashejo is a rich porage with rice. The vegetables and spices give it a wonderful flavor that knocks out any cold!
