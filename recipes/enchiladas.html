Enchiladas

Description

Enchiladas.

Ingredients

  • Tortillas
  • Ground beef
  • Cheese
  • Enchilada sauce

Steps

  1. In a pan brown the ground beef.
  2. In the pan used for the ground beef add a small amount of oil and on low warm up some tortillas.
  3. Once the tortilla is warm place into a baking dish and fill with ground beef before rolling up into an enchilada shape
  4. Continue until the baking sheet is full then add the enchilada sauce.
  5. Top with cheese and bake for 30 to 45 minutes at 350 degrees farenheit.
